MilaCoins automated crypto payment processor

== Description ==

Start accepting payments for goods or services in cryptocurrencies without risk. With MilaCoins automated crypto payment processor, you can increase your sales and offer crypto payments to millions of crypto users worldwide. All payments are settled to the wallet of your choice (fiat or crypto). No matter what crypto coin your customer chooses to pay in, you will receive your converted funds to your preferred wallet set in the currency of your choice.

== Installation ==

1. Go to plugins -> add new
2. Search for Milacoins
3. Install and activate.
3. Configure payment gateway in WooCommerce -> Settings -> Payments -> Milacoins
